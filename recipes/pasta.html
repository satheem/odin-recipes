Odin Pasta Recipe

A delicious pasta dish with tomato sauce, basil, and Parmesan cheese, perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Spaghetti, Penne, or your preferred type of pasta
  • Sauce: Tomato sauce with garlic, olive oil, and herbs
  • Cheese: Parmesan, Mozzarella
  • Herbs: Basil, oregano
  • Olive Oil: For sautéing the garlic and drizzling

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add the tomato sauce and herbs, simmering for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  5. Top with Parmesan and fresh basil.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

PIZZA

A pizza is a round, flatbread topped with sauce, cheese, and various ingredients like vegetables, meats, and herbs. It's baked until the crust is golden and crispy, with a melty, flavorful topping. Classic varieties include Margherita, Pepperoni, and Veggie.

Ingredients

  • Dough: Flour, water, yeast, salt, olive oil (for the crust)
  • Sauce: Tomato sauce (often seasoned with garlic, olive oil, and herbs like oregano)
  • Cheese: Mozzarella (the most common), along with Parmesan or other cheeses
  • Toppings:
    • Meats: Pepperoni, sausage, ham, bacon, or chicken
    • Vegetables: Mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, spinach, tomatoes
    • Herbs and Spices: Basil, oregano, garlic, red pepper flakes
  • Olive Oil: For drizzling on top or brushing the crust

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, and salt, then knead and let it rise for about 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add your preferred toppings, such as pepperoni, vegetables, or herbs.
  7. Drizzle olive oil around the crust and over the pizza.
  8. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  9. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!

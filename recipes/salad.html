Odin Salad Recipe

A fresh, healthy salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and a light vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Greens: Mixed greens, spinach, arugula
  • Vegetables: Tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers
  • Dressing: Olive oil, vinegar, salt, pepper
  • Extras: Croutons, feta cheese, olives (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables and greens.
  2. In a bowl, mix the greens and vegetables together.
  3. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper for the dressing.
  4. Toss the salad with the dressing.
  5. Top with croutons, feta, and olives (optional).
  6. Serve and enjoy!
